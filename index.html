What I Offer

Services tailored to
where you are in the process

Walk-Through Consultation

A comprehensive on-site visit — up to two hours — walking through your home together and identifying exactly what needs to change before marketing begins. Using what you already own to create the right look for photos.

Pre-Photography Prep & Styling

Hands-on styling on or before shoot day to make sure every room reads beautifully on camera. The details that matter most!

Virtual Styling

Not in Wellington, or prefer to start remotely? A video call and email follow-up covers the key principles and gives you a clear action plan.

New Home Set-Up

Moving into a new place? I'll help you arrange and style your furniture and belongings so your new home feels considered and complete from day one.

Furniture & Accessories Guidance

How It Works

A simple, considered
process from start to finish

01

Discovery Call

We start with a phone conversation to understand your home, your timeline, and what you're hoping to achieve. From there, we agree on a time that works for you.

02

On-Site Consultation

I spend up to two hours walking through your home with you — room by room — identifying what to move, edit, or add. You'll leave with a clear picture of what needs to happen.

03

Tailored Follow-Up

After the consultation, I send through visual references, example photos, and any specific recommendations — paint colours, styling notes, or pieces worth sourcing — so you have everything in one place.

04

Photography Day Optional Add-On

As an add-on, I'm available to come back on the morning of your shoot for a final styling pass before the photographer arrives.

About

Hi, I'm Tracey.

I'm a property stylist who works directly with vendors to get their home looking its absolute best for marketing photos and beyond.

What I do is a little different from traditional home staging. Rather than bringing in furniture and accessories, I work with what you already have — making the most of your existing pieces and only suggesting additional items where they'd genuinely elevate the overall look. I'm also mindful of budgets, so nothing I recommend will break the bank.

Because most buyers encounter a property online before they ever step through the door, first impressions really do matter. My focus is on how your home will read in photographs — once it looks great there, it naturally carries through to your open homes too.

After the consultation has been completed, I'll follow up with example photos so you can see exactly how those spaces will come across on camera. It's a handy reference for keeping things photo-ready on the day.

A beautifully presented home not only photographs better — it genuinely supports the agents in achieving the best possible result for your sale. As an optional add-on, I'm also happy to come back on the morning of the shoot to do a final styling pass before the photographer arrives.
